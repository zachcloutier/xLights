Sub-buffers

On the layer settings panel is the sub-buffer box (the black one with the yellow dashed rectangle). Did you know that you can:

  • Double click on the black rectangle to reset it to the default full size.
  • Hold down the control key while moving to limit movement in the horizontal direction
  • Hold down the control and shift keys while moving to limit movement in the vertical direction
  • Right click to:
    • Choose from a number of predefined sub-buffers
    • Manually edit the positions including placing value curves on the edge positions.
    • Apply the current buffer to all selected effects (like bulk edit).
