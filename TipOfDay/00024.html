VUMeter cryptic names

VU Meter has lots of inbuilt types with cryptic names - so here is your decoding guide:

  • Triggers:
    • Timing Event - These types require you to select a timing track. At the start/end of every timing interval the VU meter will trigger and take an action.
    • Level - These types use the sensitivity setting as a level (0-100) - when the audio crosses from below that level to over that level the VU meter will trigger and take an action
    • Note - Allows you to filter the range of notes the VU meter is sensitive to.
  • Actions:
    • Bar - When the trigger occurs a bar will be drawn that then moves across the element typically from right to left. The size of the bar is controlled by the bars setting.
    • Pulse - When the trigger occurs the lights will pulse on and then fade based on the bars setting.
    • Color - When the trigger occurs it will cycle to the next selected colour
    • Jump - When the trigger occurs the effect will fill the element vertically and then drop away based on the bars setting
    • Shape - Displays a shape proportional to the music intensity. Play with sensitivity to control how big the shape is relative to the audio
    • On - Turns on the LEDs proportional to the music intensity
    • Sweep - Sweep across the model
