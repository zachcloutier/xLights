Sequencer navigation shortcuts

Navigating quickly in the sequencer can save a lot of time:

  • Scroll Wheel - scrolls models up and down
  • Shift + Scroll Wheel - scrolls time left and right
  • Ctrl + Scroll Wheel - zooms in and out
  • Page up/down - scrolls models up and down
  • Up/Down arrows - moves selected effects between layers/models
  • Home - jumps to start of sequence
  • End - jumps to end of sequence
  • Ctrl + . - remembers where you current at
  • Ctrl + / - jumps back to the location you just saved with Ctrl + .
