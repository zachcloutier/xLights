Easter Eggs

xSchedule has 2 Easter Eggs in it in the form of games. To access them:

  1. You need a matrix or tree that is at least 20 pixels high and 10 wide - anything less and it just wont work.
  2. You need to setup xSchedule - at least enough to play the game.
  3. You need to describe your tree/matrix in xSchedule ... see the Edit/Matrix menu option ,,, these settings are very similar to the ones you use in xLights. If you have a 360 tree you may want to only include the front part of the tree when you define your matrix.
  4. You need to access the xSchedule web page ... ideally from a phone but you can also do it on your computer.
  5. You need to click on the blue version text at the bottom of the page to launch the easer egg ... there are actually 2 links here. Each one takes you to a different game.

Have fun and don't forget to post a video of your game play ... and maybe a high score.
