xSchedule Show Folder

Pay attention to the colour of your show folder name at the bottom left of the schedule window.

If it is red xschedule can't find your xlights_networks.xml file which it needs to know where to send the data to. This should be in your xlights show folder. When in this state xSchedule cannot control any lights.

If it is amber it means that when you last ran xlights on this machine you had it pointed at a different show folder to the one you have xschedule pointed at. This may be intentional or it may just explain why your lights look different under xlights and xschedule.

If it is black then it is the same of xLights and is almost certainly set correctly.

As ever run check schedule to get this all explained.
